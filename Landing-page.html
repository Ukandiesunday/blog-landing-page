Encouraged by her family and teachers, she pursued formal education in Fine Arts, refining her skills and expanding her knowledge. Notable projects include public art installations, collaborations with other artists, and exhibitions in various galleries. She mentors young artists and participates in community art projects, making art accessible to all.
